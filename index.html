Ferrari SF90 Stardale

Beyond Imagination

The Ferrari SF90 Stradale represents a groundbreaking leap forward in the realm of high-performance hybrid hypercars. Unveiled as Ferrari's first series-production plug-in hybrid, the SF90 Stradale seamlessly blends the raw power of a V8 internal combustion engine with three electric motors, delivering an awe-inspiring total power output of 1,000 horsepower. Its design is a masterpiece of aerodynamics, featuring sleek lines and an aggressive stance that not only enhances its visual appeal but also contributes to its exceptional performance.
